Music

There is nothing quite as magnificent as music. It has the ability to gather people from all over the world in one place, even though they dont speak the same language. It is a way to express yourself, a part of your identity.

I'm not quite sure what I would do if I couldn't enjoy music whenever I feel like it. I would be loosing one of the dearest things in my life.
